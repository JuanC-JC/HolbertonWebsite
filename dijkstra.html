Dijkstra's algorithm, also called minimum path algorithm, is an algorithm for determining the shortest path, given an origin vertex, to the rest of the vertices in a network that has weights on each edge. Its name refers to Edsger Dijkstra, a computer scientist from the Netherlands who conceived it in 1956 and first published it in 1959.
